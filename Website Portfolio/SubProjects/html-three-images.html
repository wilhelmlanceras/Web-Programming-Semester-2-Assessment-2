Two Tails, One Heart

Double Trouble, Double Love

A perfect pair—different but inseparable. Tutong’s relaxed affection meets Bahaw’s playful but cautious nature, creating a balance only they understand. Whether curled up close or playfully swatting at each other, their bond is unshakable.

Soft Eyes, Warm Heart

Tutong wears his love openly. Always close, always leaning in, he craves connection without hesitation. Whether he’s pressing against you or lounging nearby, his clingy yet laid-back nature makes him impossible to resist.

Shy Eyes, Playful Paws

Reserved but full of energy, Bahaw is a mix of curiosity and caution. She watches from a distance, playful yet hesitant—until she’s comfortable. Then, the real Bahaw comes alive, a loving shadow to those she trusts the most.
